Vastu Myths vs Facts

Separating superstition from the authentic science of Vastu Shastra.

Myth

Vastu requires complete demolition and rebuilding of existing structures.

Fact

Most Vastu corrections can be achieved through simple, non-invasive remedies like color changes, mirror placements, and yantra installations without structural modifications.

Myth

Only south-facing houses are inauspicious according to Vastu.

Fact

Every direction has positive and negative aspects. South-facing homes can be highly prosperous when designed with proper Vastu principles, especially for business owners.

Myth

Vastu is only relevant for new constructions.

Fact

Vastu remedies can be effectively applied to existing buildings through interior modifications, color therapy, and energy-balancing techniques without major renovations.
