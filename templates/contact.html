Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical architectural project take?

The timeline varies based on project scope and complexity. A residential project typically takes 6-12 months from concept to completion, while larger commercial projects may take 1-2 years. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you work with remote clients?

Yes, we regularly work with clients across India and internationally. We utilize video conferencing, digital collaboration tools, and occasional site visits to ensure seamless communication and project management regardless of location.

What is your design process?

Our process begins with understanding your needs and vision, followed by concept development, detailed design, and finally project execution. We maintain close collaboration throughout, ensuring the final result aligns with your expectations.

Can you help with obtaining permits and approvals?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience navigating local regulations and approval processes. We handle all necessary documentation and coordinate with authorities to secure required permits for your project.
